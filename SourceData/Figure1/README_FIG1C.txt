BehaviourPref is the matrix containing the behaviour for the preference-based task. 
BehaviourPerc is the matrix containing the behaviour for the perceptual task. 

They are both organised as follows: 
- each line is a trial 
- Columns: 
	1. Subject number
	2. Performance (0 = incorrect/ 1 = correct)
	3. RT (in seconds)
	4. Difficulty level (1= most difficult to 4=easiest).
